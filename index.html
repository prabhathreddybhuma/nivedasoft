Our Journey

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate technology professionals, Niveda Software Solutions has evolved into a leading provider of SAP ERP training and consulting services. With decades of collective experience working with global enterprises and Fortune 500 companies, we specialize in SAP solutions, enterprise data-center infrastructure, cloud technologies, and digital transformation enablement.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and industry practice. We combine hands‑on learning, industry‑aligned modules, and personalized mentoring to ensure every participant gains confidence and job‑ready skills that translate directly to workplace success.
