About Niveda

Pioneering SAP solutions and enterprise training since 2015

Our Journey

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate technology professionals, our training institute has proudly evolved into a leading provider of SAP ERP training. With decades of collective experience working with global enterprises and top multinational companies, we specialize in SAP solutions, enterprise data-center infrastructure, cloud technologies, and digital transformation enablement.

Our mission is to help learners and professionals bridge the gap between theory and industry practice. At our core, we believe that quality training is the foundation for successful careers. Our trainers provide personal guidance and support throughout the training journey, combining hands-on learning, industry-aligned modules, and personalized mentoring to ensure every participant gains confidence during the training.
